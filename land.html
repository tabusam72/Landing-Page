A Year of WordPress Struggles:Lessons Learned

Embarking on a journey with WordPress seemed like a promising adventure a year ago.Here’s a glimpse into my year-long struggle and the lessons I’ve learned along the way. When I first started with WordPress, I was overwhelmed by the sheer number of themes and plugins available..
Each one promised to transform my website into a digital masterpiece.I spent countless hours experimenting, only to realize that not all themes and plugins are compatible. Start with the basics and build gradually. It's better to have a simple, functional site than a complex one that breaks. My journey with WordPress has been a rollercoaster of emotions, from frustration to triumph. If you’re just starting out, remember that every expert was once a beginner. Embrace the struggles, learn from them, and never give up. Your dream website is within reach.
